####  Challenges & How I Solved Them
- **Challenge:** Distinguishing between ECS, EKS, and Fargate — all container-related but with different use cases.
- **Solution:** Built a comparison table: ECS (AWS-native orchestration), EKS (Kubernetes-based), and Fargate (serverless — no cluster management required).

####  Key Takeaways & Reflections
> AWS offers multiple compute options, and choosing the right one depends on workload type, management preference, and cost requirements. Serverless (Lambda, Fargate) removes infrastructure burden but requires a different design mindset. EC2 provides maximum control, while Elastic Beanstalk abstracts away the underlying complexity for developers.
