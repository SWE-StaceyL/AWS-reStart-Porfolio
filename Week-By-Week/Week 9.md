Challenges & How I Solved Them

Challenge: Keeping track of the many AI/ML services and their specific use cases — Rekognition, Comprehend, Textract, Kendra, and Polly can seem similar at first glance.
Solution: Created a one-line summary for each: Rekognition = images & video; Comprehend = text analysis & NLP; Textract = extract data from documents; Kendra = intelligent search; Polly = text to speech; Personalize = recommendations; SageMaker = build, train & deploy custom ML models.

Key Takeaways & Reflections
Week 9 revealed just how accessible machine learning has become on AWS. Rather than building models from scratch, AWS provides high-level AI services for nearly every use case — vision, language, search, speech, and personalisation. SageMaker sits at the centre for teams that need custom model development. Understanding which service fits which problem is the real skill here, and it is one that directly supports Solutions Architect thinking.
